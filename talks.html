Tier 1 — Talks (45–90 minutes)

Low-risk intros for meetups, conferences, or internal sessions. Each talk stands alone.

Confidence Is Not a Safety Mechanism

How pressure manufactures certainty — and how to keep decisions safe when the room gets confident too fast.

More Data Won’t Save You

Why “more data” often accelerates bad decisions. Introduces admissibility: what evidence is allowed to justify action.

Why Smart Systems Fail Under Pressure

What changes in decision environments when stakes rise — and why good systems start amplifying risk.

When Agreement Becomes a Risk

Why consensus can hide uncertainty and diffuse ownership at exactly the wrong moment.

Tier 2 — Workshops (half-day)

Applied sessions built around one real decision your team is facing. The goal is not perfect certainty — it’s safer posture.

Decision Posture (Applied)

Work one decision end-to-end: admissibility → constraints → triggers → ownership. Leave with an artifact you can reuse.

Upward Enforcement

How to preserve structure when leadership pressure appears — without escalating politics or turning it into “pushback.”

Bottom-Up Adoption Without Theater

Start with one decision and a few safe moves. No framework rollout. No evangelism. No organizational immune response.

Data Admissibility Clinic

Teach your team to distinguish “true data” from “decision-allowed data.” Prevent metrics from becoming authority laundering.

Tier 3 — Flagship (2 days)

The full cohort experience. Designed for senior ICs, architects, product leaders, and executives who repeatedly face irreversible decisions.

  • Structured exercises that pressure-test posture in a safe environment
  • Reusable decision templates and facilitation phrases
  • Admissibility, constraints, triggers, ownership — practiced, not theorized
